Ferheng

Modern Kurmanji Dictionary

Ferheng is a modern Kurmanji dictionary designed to make learning and using the Kurdish language easier. With its modern features and simple design, it supports both learners and native speakers who want to strengthen their Kurdish.

Designed for Everyone

The app was designed with multi-language support in mind, because many Kurds grow up in countries where Kurdish is not officially taught, or are born abroad. While they may understand basic Kurdish, they are often more fluent in another language that they learned in school.
